About us

The Computer Science Engineering Association(CSEA) was established with the primary goal of knowledge advancement.

Being the brainchild of Department of Computer Science and Engineering(DCSE) of CEG, one of the oldest Engineering schools of the country, the association ensures that the role of technocrats and specialists in various industries, is implied.

It functions to develop and promote the progression ofÂ  Computer Science and technologies to its students as well as the members of the global community through its rich alumni support. Comprising students and faculty, the organization is one of the most active Engineering Associations in the city.

Events

Sync

The intra-college level symposium, identifies and facilitates the talents from within the college through a variety of events organized by the students. With the events being online as well as onsite, the one day symposium is a launching platform for many budding Computer Engineers.

aBaCUS

The activities of the association culminate with the annual technical symposium- ABACUS. With each year's growing levels of success, it attracts about 400 students from all over the country to attend various workshops, paper presentations and interactive sessions. The events also include professional seminars for companies and institutions that are interested and looking for efficient engineers, providing phenomenal opportunities to young minds seeking for internships. The association with a round the clock mechanism helps students in various others aspects granting a prospectus for all of them to showcase their talents.

Vision

Engineering is a rewarding career. CSEA's mission is to provide updated resources and a highly developed structure to capacitate an interactive environment between the students and faculty.

The association also attempts to conduct classes on various topics for the students with the classes being taken by the students, hence can rightly be stated the association by the students and for the students.
